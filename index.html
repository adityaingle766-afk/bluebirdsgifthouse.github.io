    About Us

    🏪 Our Story

    Welcome to Bluebirds Gift House — your one-stop destination for thoughtful gifts, creative stationery, and everything that makes moments memorable. We are a proudly local gift shop based in Akot, Maharashtra, serving customers with love since our establishment.

    At Bluebirds Gift House, we believe that a well-chosen gift speaks a thousand words. Whether it's a beautifully crafted photo frame, a fun toy for a child, an inspiring book, or a set of quality stationery — we have something for every occasion and every budget.

    🛍️ What We Sell

    • Photo Frames — single, collage, digital, and decorative
    • Gift Toys — for children and adults of all ages
    • Books — story books, educational, activity books
    • Stationery — pens, notebooks, planners, art supplies
    • Gift Hampers and Combos for all occasions
    • Decorative items and novelty gifts
    • Festival special gift collections

    Refund & Cancellation Policy
    Last updated: March 2025

    ❌ Order Cancellation

    You may cancel within 24 hours of placing, if not dispatched. Customised items cannot be cancelled once production begins.
